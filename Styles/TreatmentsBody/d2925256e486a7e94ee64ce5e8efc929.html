    Vein Reduction

    Sclerotherapy with DrMinx - Smooth, Clear Legs with Long-Lasting Results

    Varicose veins and spider veins are common concerns that can affect not only the appearance but also the comfort of your legs. At DrMinx Aesthetics, we offer sclerotherapy, an effective, non-surgical solution to permanently eliminate unwanted veins, leaving you with clear, smooth skin and improved leg health.

    What Causes Varicose and Spider Veins?

    Varicose and spider veins occur when veins become damaged or weakened, causing blood to pool and resulting in:

    - Visible, bulging veins that appear purple or blue.
    - Red, thread-like veins near the surface of the skin (spider veins).
    - Swelling, heaviness, and discomfort in the affected area.

    These veins can develop due to factors like genetics, pregnancy, weight changes, and prolonged sitting or standing.
    While not a serious health concern, they can affect appearance and self-confidence.
    How Sclerotherapy Works - A Proven Solution

    Dr. Minoka Nadesan uses sclerotherapy to treat spider and varicose veins. The process involves injecting a solution directly into the veins, causing them to collapse and fade. This treatment:

    - Permanently eliminates spider and varicose veins.
    - Restores smoother, even-toned skin by improving leg appearance.
    - Improves circulation by redirecting blood flow to healthier veins.

    Why Choose Sclerotherapy?

    - Non-surgical treatment - Minimal discomfort with no downtime.
    - Immediate results - Results begin to show after the first session, with treated veins starting to fade.
    - Long-lasting results - The improvements are permanent, and the veins won't return.

    Multiple Sessions Required
    While results are visible after just one session, 4 sessions are typically required for most patients, depending on the severity of the veins. Severe cases may require additional treatments to ensure complete and permanent elimination of the veins. Since varicose veins and spider veins can be reperfused (reopen) over time, multiple sessions are needed to ensure lasting results.

    What to Expect?

    - 4 sessions are typically required, spaced over a few weeks.
    - Results from the first session will be noticeable, with further improvement after subsequent treatments.
    - No downtime - Return to normal activities immediately.

    - Fashion-forward design - Stylish and comfortable for daily wear.

    Permanent Laser Hair Removal

    Laser Hair Removal with DrMinx - Get Hair-Free and Confident!

Our InMode Diode Laser technology is designed to give you smooth, hair-free skin by targeting and permanently removing unwanted hair.     How Does InMode Diode Laser Work?

    The InMode Diode Laser uses focused light energy to target the hair follicle at its root. Over time, this process leads to permanent hair loss, with treated follicles being unable to produce new hair.

    - Permanently reduces hair growth - Targeting and destroying the follicle.
    - Safe for all skin types - Effectively works on both light and dark skin tones.

    Treatment Frequency

    For optimal results, laser hair removal treatments should be done once a month for 6 to 8 sessions. This schedule ensures that all hair growth cycles are treated effectively, as hair grows in different phases, and only hair in the active growth phase can be targeted.

    Excessive Sweating

    Excessive Sweating Treatment with DrMinx – Regain Control and Confidence

    Excessive sweating, also known as hyperhidrosis, can be an uncomfortable and embarrassing condition that affects your daily life. Whether it's underarm sweating, hand sweating, or foot sweating, DrMinx offers an effective solution using botulinum toxin (Botox) to provide relief and restore your comfort and confidence.

    How Does Botox Work for Excessive Sweating?

    Botox is a safe and FDA-approved treatment that works by blocking the signals from the nerves to the sweat glands. This prevents the glands from producing excessive sweat, allowing you to stay dry and comfortable. The treatment is non-invasive, quick, and offers lasting results.

    - Mechanism: Botox works by inhibiting the release of acetylcholine, a neurotransmitter that triggers the sweat glands. By stopping the communication between nerves and glands, sweating is significantly reduced.

    - Target Areas: Botox can be injected into underarms, palms, feet, and other areas prone to excessive sweating.

    Treatment and Frequency

    For optimal results, Botox injections need to be repeated twice a year. This ensures that sweat production stays under control, and you continue to enjoy the benefits of dry skin.

    Why Choose Botox for Excessive Sweating?

    - Safe and FDA-approved - Botox is an FDA-approved treatment option for hyperhidrosis, ensuring its safety and effectiveness.
    - Quick and effective - Botox injections are a quick procedure, with minimal downtime, providing relief in just a few days.
    - Long-lasting results - Treatment lasts up to 6 months, with results improving as the body adjusts to the therapy.
    - Minimal discomfort - Botox injections are minimally invasive, offering little to no discomfort during the procedure.
    - Life-changing - Botox for sweating can significantly improve your quality of life, giving you the freedom to wear the clothes you want and feel more comfortable in everyday situations.

    Don't let excessive sweating control your life.

    Post Operation Scar Reduction

    Post Liposuction and Post-Surgery with DrMinx

    Healing from procedures like liposuction or tummy tucks can often result in scarring, and post-surgical fluid accumulation can be painful. At DrMinx Aesthetics, we've created a comprehensive treatment program designed to reduce scarring, promote skin rejuvenation, and accelerate healing through a combination of Morpheus, radio wave therapy, and lymphatic drainage. This integrated approach ensures a painless, relaxing, and effective recovery process, helping you regain smooth, even skin while alleviating post-operative discomfort.

    Morpheus for Scar Reduction and Skin Rejuvenation

    Morpheus8 is a cutting-edge microneedling radiofrequency (RF) treatment that works by stimulating collagen production deep within the skin. This makes it an ideal solution for reducing scars and rejuvenating the skin following surgery. The combination of microneedling with radiofrequency energy works to break down excess collagen in scar tissue, while stimulating the formation of new, smoother skin.

    How It Works for Scarring

    Morpheus is especially effective for post-surgical scars, including those from tummy tucks and liposuction. By targeting the deeper layers of the skin, it helps to:

    - Flatten raised scars and soften keloid tissue
    - Stimulate collagen production for smoother, more even skin texture
    - Improve skin elasticity and rejuvenate the area

    Treatment Plan and Frequency

    For optimal results, we recommend 3 to 4 sessions of Morpheus spaced 2 to 3 months apart, depending on the severity and type of scar. Since Morpheus promotes the gradual remodeling of scar tissue, it is most effective when performed at least 6 months after surgery to allow for proper healing.

    Package Options

    We offer personalized scar treatment packages based on your specific needs and goals. These packages are tailored to address your unique condition, and we will discuss pricing during your consultation.

    Post-Operative Fluid Drainage with Radio Wave Therapy

    After liposuction or other body contouring procedures, patients often experience fluid buildup in the treatment areas. This can cause pain, discomfort, and prolonged swelling. To alleviate these symptoms, DrMinx has invested in a state-of-the-art radio wave device, which is used for painless lymphatic drainage and post-op sculpting.

    How It Works

    The radio wave device uses gentle, non-invasive radiofrequency waves to stimulate the lymphatic system, facilitating the removal of excess fluid and reducing swelling. This therapy not only helps in accelerating the healing process but also improves skin texture and contours after surgery.

    - Painless and relaxing - Unlike traditional manual massage, this device provides a relaxing experience while reducing discomfort and pain.
    - Immediate post-op care - Radio wave therapy can be used immediately after surgery to reduce swelling and prevent fluid retention.
    - Accelerated healing - By promoting drainage and improving circulation, this treatment accelerates the body's natural healing process, helping you recover faster.

    When is Radio Wave Therapy Used?

    Radio wave therapy can be used immediately post-surgery to address swelling and fluid retention. Morpheus for scar reduction will be done later, once your skin has sufficiently healed, typically 6 months after surgery.
