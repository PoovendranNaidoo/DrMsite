About Us

DrMinx medical spa is a cosmetic institute that offers services from basic cosmetic requirements to a more advanced and minimal invasive aesthetic procedures. The medical spa boosts a state of the art facility with cutting edge aesthetic procedures and equipment, it is one of the few medical institutes that houses a hyperbaric chamber. 

The institute offers a professional service that is at the the forefront of aesthetic medicine this ensures that the patients are guaranteed to receive a high quality end result embraced with a personal touch. DRMinx medical spa's core principles are centered around offering our patients a memorable experience whilst maintaining the highest code of medical ethics.

DR MINOKA NADESAN

Founder of DRMinx Medical spa
'Minx 'started merely as a high school nickname and soon became an enduring link between doctor and patient. DrMinx is Pretoria's Key Opinion Leader for Juverderm Filler, Sinclair silhouette threads and the cosmeceutical product range Neostrata . She is a national trainer of the prestigious training academy, Allergan Medical Institute in South Africa, which promotes aesthetics excellence by training the South African doctors. DrMinx has featured in a host of South African publications Sunday times, Sowetan, Marie Claire, Huisgenoot, A2 Magazine, to name but a few. 

Being a mom, wife and entrepreneur DrMinx realised that time is often limited, her vision is to create a friendly safe and tranquil space that would offer her patients the opportunity to have all their aesthetic needs taken care of within one setting.
