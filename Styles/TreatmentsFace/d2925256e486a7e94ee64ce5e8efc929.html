    Filler with DrMinx

    Filler Treatments - Tailored to Your Unique Needs

    As we age, volume loss, sagging, and deep lines become more noticeable. Dermal fillers are an effective, non-surgical solution to restore youthful contours, smooth wrinkles, and enhance facial harmony. At DrMinx Aesthetics, we use both Hyaluronic Acid (HA) fillers and Collagen-Stimulating (Bio-Stimulatory) fillers, depending on your unique needs.

    Types of Fillers: HA vs. Collagen-Stimulating

    DrMinx follows a clear representation system to help patients understand the difference between filler types:

    ✔ HA Fillers = Clear Representation - These fillers provide instant hydration, volume, and definition but require maintenance every 12 months.
    ✔ Collagen-Stimulating Fillers = White Representation - These fillers work deep within the skin to trigger your body's own collagen production, offering longer-lasting, natural-looking results over time. They are more expensive but provide semi-permanent results that improve skin texture, elasticity, and lift.

    Which Filler is Right for You?

    DrMinx will assess your full face holistically and recommend the best treatment plan based on your concerns:

    ✔ Eye Bags & Dark Circles - HA filler restores volume loss under the eyes for a refreshed, youthful look.
    ✔ Nasolabial Folds & Marionette Lines - Fillers smooth deep lines around the mouth for a softer, lifted appearance.
    ✔ Jowls & Lower Face Sagging - Collagen-stimulating fillers help redefine the jawline and lift the lower face naturally.
    ✔ Double Chin & Facial Contouring - Strategic filler placement enhances definition and balances facial proportions.

    Why Choose DrMinx for Fillers?

    ✔ Customized Treatment Plan - Every patient receives a tailored approach that enhances natural beauty without overfilling.
    ✔ Full Face Assessment - We focus on harmony and balance, ensuring results blend seamlessly.
    ✔ Long-Lasting, Natural Results - Whether HA or collagen-stimulating fillers, results are always subtle yet transformative.

    Non Surgical Rhinoplasty with DrMinx

    Nose Threads - A Safe & Effective Alternative to Fillers

    For those looking to enhance their nose shape without surgery, nose threads are a safe and effective option that provide natural-looking lift and definition. Unlike fillers, which add volume, threads work by lifting and refining the nose structure, creating a slimmer, more sculpted bridge and improving overall facial harmony.

    How Do Nose Threads Work?

    ✔ Lifts & Slims – Threads are placed along the nasal bridge to create a higher, more defined structure without widening the nose.
    ✔ Enhances Tip Projection – Threads can subtly lift the nasal tip, improving the profile and creating a more refined, elegant look.
    ✔ Collagen Stimulation – Over time, threads encourage collagen production, further enhancing the nose’s structure naturally.
    ✔ Long-Lasting Results – Results typically last 12-18 months, with improvements seen immediately after treatment.

    Why Choose Nose Threads Over Fillers?

    ✔ Safer Option – Unlike fillers, which can pose vascular risks in the nose, threads are a low-risk alternative.
    ✔ No Overfilling – Fillers can sometimes make the nose appear wider, while threads create a more structured, lifted look.
    ✔ Minimal Downtime – The procedure is quick, minimally invasive, and requires little recovery.

    Is This the Right Treatment for You?

    DrMinx will assess your nasal structure and facial proportions to determine whether nose threads are the best option for achieving your desired look.

    ✔ Ideal for those wanting a higher nasal bridge, a slimmer nose, or subtle tip enhancement
    ✔ Not suitable for severe nose asymmetries or structural deviations

    Lumecca with DrMinx

    Lumecca - The Ultimate IPL Treatment for Clear, Radiant Skin

    Lumecca is a high-powered IPL (Intense Pulsed Light) treatment specifically designed to target pigmentation, sun damage, and vascular lesions for brighter, clearer skin. It is one of the most advanced IPL systems available, delivering superior results in fewer sessions.

    Who Is Lumecca For?

    ✔ Best suited for light skin tones - Lumecca is highly effective on Fitzpatrick skin types I-III (lighter complexions) and is not recommended for darker skin tones due to the risk of hyperpigmentation.
    ✔ Most popular treatment for men - Lumecca is a top choice for male patients looking to clear sun damage, broken capillaries, and uneven skin tone without downtime.

    What Can Lumecca Treat?

    ✔ Hyperpigmentation & Dark Spots - Fades age spots, sun damage, and uneven skin tone for a brighter complexion.
    ✔ Rosacea & Redness - Targets broken capillaries and redness, improving skin clarity.
    ✔ Sun Damage & Freckles - Clears photo-damage and restores a youthful glow.
    ✔ Acne Scarring - Helps fade post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation (PIH).
    ✔ General Skin Rejuvenation - Boosts collagen production, leaving skin firmer and more refreshed.

    Why Lumecca?

    ✔ 3x More Powerful Than Standard IPL - Delivers faster, better results in fewer sessions.
    ✔ Minimal Downtime - Mild redness fades within hours, allowing quick recovery.
    ✔ Quick & Effective - Full-face treatment takes just 20 minutes.

    How Many Sessions Are Needed?

    While some patients see results after one session, 2-3 sessions are usually recommended for optimal correction.

    What Causes Under-Eye Bags?

    ✔ Aging - As we age, fat pads around the eyes can shift, causing the skin to sag and leading to the formation of under-eye bags and hollows.

    MesoBrightening Injections & Brightening Peels: DrMinx's Comprehensive Pigmentation Solution

    At DrMinx Aesthetics, we tackle pigmentation from every angle. By combining MesoBrightening Injections with Brightening Peels, we address both the immediate and long-term causes of pigmentation, providing a comprehensive solution to achieve a brighter, more even complexion. Our focus is not just on reducing pigmentation, but also on preventing future pigmentation with DrMinx's exclusive Triple Protection Method.

    Why MesoBrightening Injections & Brightening Peels?

    Pigmentation often arises from sun exposure, oxidative stress, and inflammatory skin conditions, so effective treatment must address both the visible pigmentation and the skin's overall health. DrMinx's MesoBrightening Injections and Brightening Peels are designed to reduce existing pigmentation and restore balance, using a combination of antioxidants, niacinamide, and depigmenting agents. Together, they work synergistically to brighten and renew your skin.

    How MesoBrightening Injections Work

    Targeted Ingredients - The MesoBrightening Injections use a powerful cocktail of antioxidants, niacinamide, and brightening agents to reduce pigmentation from within and brighten the skin.

    Fast-Acting Results - Visible improvements in pigmentation are seen after a few sessions, helping to achieve a clear, even complexion.

    Precision Treatment - The injections are carefully applied to the targeted areas to address stubborn pigmentation, ensuring a personalized and effective treatment plan.

    How Brightening Peels Work

    Exfoliation and Renewal - The Brightening Peels exfoliate the skin's surface, removing dead cells and promoting the renewal of healthy, even-toned skin.

    Brightening Effect - The peel helps to lighten dark spots and even out skin tone, giving the skin a radiant, glowing appearance.

    Stimulates Collagen - In addition to reducing pigmentation, the peel stimulates collagen production, improving the overall texture and elasticity of the skin.

    DrMinx's Triple Protection Method for Long-Term Results
